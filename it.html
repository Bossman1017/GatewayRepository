The I.T. Scene

In the past 12 months, Dallas-based companies posted nearly 134,000 tech jobs. In addition, the number of IT jobs is expected to grow by 10% over the next 5 years. The top two most in-demand jobs posted in Dallas are software development engineers and Java developers. And, IT pros make a healthy median salary of $93,058 in the "Big D".

Companies

AppShark Software

AppShark Software is a mobile app and custom software developer that offers CRM consulting and system integration (SI). Founded in 2007, this 50+ team is based in Dallas, TX with offices in New Jersey and Raidurg, India.

AppShark developed enterprise resource planning (ERP) to improve the client's Salesforce functionality by combining Force.com apps and custom software. The client appreciated the team's patience and excellent value for the price.

Network Elites

Network Elites is an IT services company based in Dallas with an office in Austin, Texas. Since 2001, their team of 10 has been specializing in IT managed services, IT strategy consulting, and cloud consulting & SI for clients in the healthcare, legal, and energy industries.

Network Elites provided cybersecurity for a healthcare company concerned about the health of its website. They secured the company's website on a strategic and technical level and provide crisis consulting. The client thought they offered a reasonable service.

Ascendix Technologies

Ascendix Technologies is a development agency with offices in Kharkov, Ukraine, and Dallas. Founded in 1996, its team of more than 80 employees specializes in CRM consulting, ERP consulting, and IT managed services.

Ascendix Technologies assisted a commercial real estate company with a CRM software adoption, which allowed data to become more accessible in the system. It also helped the company track sales and properties more efficiently. The client praised the agency on being very receptive.
